About Me

Engineer. Builder.
Problem Solver.

From the moment I joined a bank as an Android Developer, one thing always bothered me — transactions are everywhere, but finding the right one feels like detective work.

That frustration, lived from the inside of a banking app team, stayed with me through 13+ years of mobile engineering — and eventually led me to build SAI, an open-source intelligent transaction search engine.

Today I serve as Lead Engineer – Android at Boubyan Bank, Kuwait, one of the region's leading digital banking platforms, where I lead a full app revamp to MVI architecture with Jetpack Compose and a modular multi-module design system.

I believe great software is built collaboratively — through clear communication with product, design, security, and DevOps teams. My role is as much about people as it is about code.
